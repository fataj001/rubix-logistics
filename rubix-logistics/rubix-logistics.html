About Rubix Logistics

A Multidisciplinary Firm Built for Nigeria

Rubix Logistics Ltd (RC: 1258117) is a privately-owned corporation specializing in Architecture, Facilities Management, Design, and Construction. Since our founding in 2015, we have supervised residential buildings, managed waste, led civil engineering projects, and delivered technology solutions for clients ranging from international NGOs to government agencies across Nigeria.

Our team of Architects, Civil, Mechanical and Electrical Engineers, Geologists, and Quantity Surveyors bring 8–38 years of combined experience — accessible, collaborative professionals who tailor every engagement to the specific demands of each unique client.
